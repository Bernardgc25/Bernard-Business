Alteration

(Focus on making what you already own look great)

Love your clothes, but not the fit? We can fix that. Our expert alterations service adjusts your existing wardrobe to fit you flawlessly. Bring in anything from a suit to a pair of jeans, and we’ll make sure it looks like it was made for you.

We commonly work on:

  • Hemming pants, skirts, and dresses
  • Taking in or letting out seams for a better fit
  • Adjusting waistbands and darts
  • Shortening or lengthening sleeves
  • Waist Adjustments

Repairs

(Focus on saving and restoring your favorite items)

Don't toss a favorite piece! We can mend, repair, and restore your clothing to give it a new lease on life. From simple fixes to more complex repairs, we handle it with care.

Common repairs include:

  • Replacing Zippers
  • Replace/Sewing Buttons
  • Patching Holes & Tears
  • Reinforcing Seams

Custom Tailoring

(Focus on creating unique, made-to-measure garments)

We create clothing that is uniquely yours. Choose your favorite fabric, and we'll guide you through the style details to design a suit or dress that perfectly suits your body and personal style. It’s more than clothing—it’s a garment made just for you.

Our custom tailoring services include:

  • Personalized fittings and consultations
  • Designing garments to your specifications
  • Choosing fabrics and materials
  • Creating suits, dresses, shirts, and more
