Alteration

(Focus on making what you already own look great)

Love your clothes, but not the fit? We can fix that. Our expert alterations service adjusts your existing wardrobe to fit you flawlessly. Bring in anything from a suit to a pair of jeans, and we’ll make sure it looks like it was made for you.

We commonly work on:

  • Hemming pants, skirts, and dresses
  • Taking in or letting out seams for a better fit
  • Adjusting waistbands and darts
  • Shortening or lengthening sleeves
  • Waist Adjustments
